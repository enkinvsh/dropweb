## What Dropweb does

Dropweb is a VPN client. It creates an encrypted VPN tunnel on the user's device and routes traffic according to the user's selected subscription profile.

## Data processed by the app

1. VPN subscription URLs and imported proxy profiles.
2. App settings needed to run the VPN client.
3. Device or other IDs sent as provider request headers when fetching subscription and cabinet data. This includes HWID or device headers retained by owner decision.
4. Connection diagnostics needed to troubleshoot VPN operation, only when the user or support flow sends them.

## Data not sold

Dropweb does not sell personal data.
