# VpnService Demo Video Script

Status: owner recording required.

## Goal

Record a short reviewer video that proves VPN is the core purpose of Dropweb.

## Script

1. Show the app icon and launch Dropweb.
2. Show the main dashboard identifying Dropweb as a VPN client.
3. Import or select the demo subscription profile.
4. Tap the main connect control.
5. Show the Android VPN permission dialog.
6. Accept the dialog.
7. Show connected state and foreground VPN notification.
8. Open settings and show privacy policy, support, and account deletion links.
9. Stop the VPN from the app.

## Recording requirements

1. Use a clean device or emulator with no private notifications visible.
2. Do not show production credentials.
3. Save the final video URL in the Play Console declaration.
